# KelionAI v2 - User Manual & FAQ

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Visit [kelionai.app](https://kelionai.app)
2. Click **Sign Up** on the login page
3. Enter your email, name, and password
4. You will receive a **7-day free trial** with 10 minutes of AI interaction per day

### Logging In
1. Go to [kelionai.app](https://kelionai.app)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

## Chat Interface

### Talking to KelionAI
- Type your message in the input field at the bottom of the chat
- Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button
- The AI will respond with streaming text in real-time

### Choosing Your Avatar
- **Kelion** (male, analytical personality) - click the **K** button on the left of the avatar
- **Kira** (female, empathetic personality) - click the **K** button on the right of the avatar

### Voice Chat (MIC Button)
1. Click the **MIC** button to start recording
2. Speak your message clearly
3. Click **MIC** again to stop recording
4. Your speech is transcribed (Whisper AI) and sent to the AI
5. The AI responds with voice (ElevenLabs TTS) and the avatar lip-syncs

### Camera (CAM Button)
1. Click the **CAM** button to activate the camera
2. The camera captures a frame silently (no live preview shown)
3. The image is analyzed by GPT-4 Vision
4. The AI describes what it sees and provides assistance

### Presentation Monitor
- The left panel shows the Presentation Monitor
- When the AI generates code, charts, or visual content, it appears here
- Camera captures also display in this area

### Conversation History
- Click the **History** button in the header to view past conversations
- Click any conversation to resume it
- Create new conversations to start fresh topics

### Editing & Deleting Messages
- Hover over any message to see edit/delete options
- Click the pencil icon to edit a message
- Click the trash icon to delete a message

1. Go to **Profile** > **Subscription**

- **Monthly subscriptions**: No refunds
- **Annual subscriptions**: Refund available if less than 3 months have elapsed. Current month is deducted, remaining balance refunded within 15 working days

## FAQ

### Q: Why can't I hear the AI's voice?
A: Make sure your device volume is turned up and your browser allows audio playback. Try clicking anywhere on the page first (browsers require user interaction before playing audio).

### Q: The camera isn't working?
A: Ensure you've granted camera permissions in your browser. Check Settings > Privacy > Camera in your browser.

### Q: My free trial expired, what now?
A: Subscribe to a Pro or Enterprise plan to continue using KelionAI. Visit the Pricing page.

### Q: Can I use KelionAI on my phone?
A: Yes! KelionAI is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On mobile, you can add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

### Q: How do I add KelionAI to my home screen?
A: On iOS Safari: tap Share > Add to Home Screen. On Android Chrome: tap the menu > Add to Home Screen.

### Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). We use Supabase PostgreSQL with Row Level Security. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt.

### Q: How do I contact support?
A: Use the Contact page in the app or email contact@kelionai.app

### Q: Can I delete my account?
A: Contact the administrator at contact@kelionai.app to request account deletion.

## Troubleshooting

### App not loading
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge recommended)
- Check your internet connection

### Voice not recording
- Allow microphone permissions in browser settings
- Use HTTPS (required for microphone access)
- Try a different browser
