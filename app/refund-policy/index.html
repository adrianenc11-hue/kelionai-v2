Refund Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026 · Version 1.0

💡 Our commitment: We want you to be fully satisfied with KelionAI. If you're not happy with your subscription, we offer a straightforward refund process.

1. Eligibility for Refund

You may request a full refund under the following conditions:

  • Within 14 days of your initial subscription purchase (first-time subscribers only)
  • Service unavailability — if KelionAI was unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours during your billing period
  • Unauthorized charges — if you were charged without your consent
  • Duplicate charges — if you were charged more than once for the same subscription

2. Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly subscriptions (Pro at €9.99/month, Premium at €19.99/month):

  • You may cancel at any time from your account settings or the Stripe billing portal
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access until then
  • Refunds for the current month are available within 14 days of the charge date
  • No refunds are provided for partial months after the 14-day window

3. Annual Subscriptions

For annual subscriptions (Pro at €99.90/year, Premium at €199.90/year):

  • Full refund within the first 30 days of the annual subscription
  • Pro-rated refund available within the first 90 days, calculated based on the remaining months
  • After 90 days, no refunds are provided — you may cancel to prevent renewal
  • Cancellation stops auto-renewal; you retain access until the end of the annual period

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Free plan usage (no charges to refund)
  • Referral bonus periods (7-day Pro trials earned through referral codes)
  • Promotional or discounted subscriptions (unless stated otherwise in the promotion terms)
  • Subscriptions where the user has violated our Terms of Service

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at support@kelionai.app with the subject line "Refund Request"
  • Include your account email address and the date of the charge
  • Describe the reason for your refund request
  • We will review your request and respond within 3 business days

6. Refund Processing

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method (credit/debit card)
  • Processing time: 5–10 business days depending on your bank
  • You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued
  • Your subscription will be downgraded to the Free plan upon refund

7. Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank. Chargebacks result in additional fees and may lead to your account being suspended. We are committed to resolving any billing issues promptly and fairly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of KelionAI after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this refund policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@kelionai.app
  • Website: kelionai.app
