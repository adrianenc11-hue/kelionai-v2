🌟 What is KelionAI?

KelionAI is an advanced AI assistant with a 3D avatar that you can interact with through text or voice. Kelion learns about you over time, remembers your preferences, and can help with a wide range of tasks — from answering questions to generating images, searching the web, checking weather, and much more.

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Visit kelionai.app
  2. Type or speak to Kelion — no account needed for basic use
  3. Create a free account to unlock memory and conversation history
  4. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited access and voice features

🎛️ Chat Interface Buttons

Here's what every button in the chat interface does:

🎤
Microphone Press to start voice input. Press again to stop. Kelion will transcribe and respond.
📎
Attach File Upload images for AI vision analysis. Kelion can describe, analyze, or extract text.
🔍
Web Search Toggle real-time web search. When active, Kelion searches the internet for current info.
🖼️
Image Generation Toggle AI image creation. Describe what you want and Kelion generates it.
🌐
Translate Toggle translation mode. Kelion translates your messages to any language.
📷
Camera / Barcode Scan barcodes or take photos for AI analysis.
🔊
Voice Output Toggle Kelion's voice responses. When active, Kelion speaks the answers aloud.
📤
Send Message Send your typed message to Kelion. Also works with Enter key.

🎤 Voice Interaction

You can interact with Kelion entirely by voice:

  1. Press the 🎤 microphone button
  2. Speak your question or command clearly
  3. Press the microphone again to stop
  4. Kelion will process your speech and respond
  5. If 🔊 voice output is enabled, Kelion will also speak the answer
