What is {{APP_NAME}}?

{{APP_NAME}} is an AI-powered assistant with a 3D avatar that you can interact with through text or voice. It supports any language automatically — just write or speak in your preferred language and the assistant responds in the same language.

{{APP_NAME}} can answer questions, hold conversations, search the web in real time, generate images, translate text, analyse photos, check the weather, and remember your preferences over time.

Getting Started

  1. Open {{APP_NAME}} in your browser — no installation required
  2. Type a message in the chat box or simply speak — the microphone and camera start automatically
  3. You can use basic features without an account (Free tier: 10 chats/day)
  4. Click Login to create an account and unlock conversation history and memory
  5. Upgrade to Pro via the Plans button for higher limits and premium features

Choosing Your Avatar

{{APP_NAME}} offers two 3D avatar personas you can switch between at any time:

🧑
Kelion (Male) The default avatar. Friendly, knowledgeable, and always ready to help.
👩
Kira (Female) An alternative persona with her own voice and personality style.

To switch avatars, use the Kelion / Kira buttons located directly below the 3D avatar. Your preference is saved automatically.

Top Navigation Bar

The buttons along the top of the screen:

✉️
Invite Generate a referral code or send an invite email to a friend. Only visible when logged in.
📖
Manual Opens this user manual page.
🕓
History Opens a sidebar with your past conversations. Click any conversation to reopen it. Requires an account.
🔑
Login / Logout Sign in with your email to unlock all features, or sign out of your current session.

Voice Interaction

The microphone and camera are always active by default — no need to press any button. Just speak naturally:

  • The AI detects when you are speaking to it and responds automatically
  • Background noise and side conversations are ignored
  • Responses come as both text in the chat and spoken audio from the avatar
  • Use the Cam and Mic buttons at the bottom to toggle them on or off
  • Say "what do you see?" to ask the AI about what the camera sees

Conversation History

Click History in the top bar to open a sidebar with your past conversations:

  • Browse previous chats organised by date
  • Click on any conversation to reopen it and continue where you left off
  • Click New to start a fresh conversation
  • Each conversation is saved automatically when you are signed in

Note: History requires a registered account. Guest chats are not saved.

Account & Settings

Click your role badge (e.g. Free or User) in the top bar to access your Settings page, where you can:

  • Change your preferred language (English, Romanian, French, German, Spanish, Italian)
  • Toggle browser notifications and UI sounds
  • View your current subscription plan
  • Access the Developer portal for API integrations

Tips & Tricks

  • Be specific — The more detail you provide, the better the response
  • Use follow-ups — The AI remembers context within the same conversation. Ask "tell me more" or "explain that differently"
  • Upload files — Click the + button to upload images, PDFs, or other files for the AI to analyse
  • Multilingual — Write or speak in any language. The AI automatically detects and responds in the same language
  • Keyboard shortcut — Press Enter to send a message
  • Paste images — You can paste an image directly from your clipboard (Ctrl+V) and it will be sent to the AI
  • Drag & drop — Drag a file onto the chat to upload it
