Cookie Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026 · Version 1.0

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use KelionAI, and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication, session management, and security. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the service to function.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your language, theme, avatar preferences, and voice settings to provide a personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with KelionAI, which features are most used, and where we can improve. We use privacy-friendly analytics.
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor page load times, API response speeds, and service health to maintain optimal performance.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use minimal third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database services
  • Stripe: Payment processing (only on payment pages)
  • Google Fonts: Typography delivery

4. Cookie Consent

When you first visit KelionAI, a cookie consent banner is displayed. You can choose to accept all cookies or only essential ones. Your consent preference is stored in our database and can be changed at any time through the Settings page.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent KelionAI from functioning correctly.

6. Data Retention

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies (preferences, consent) are retained for up to 12 months. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to access, modify, or delete your cookie data. Contact us at privacy@kelionai.app for any cookie-related requests.

8. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
