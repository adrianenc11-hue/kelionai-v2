3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain the KelionAI service
  • Personalize your AI avatar experience and memory
  • Process subscriptions and payments securely via Stripe

  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (EU servers)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • AI Providers (Google, OpenAI, etc.) — For AI responses only, no personal identifiers sent
  • Railway — Application hosting

5. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — Request a copy of all your personal data
  • Right to Rectification — Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Right to Data Portability — Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object — Object to data processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@kelionai.app. We respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained while account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Chat history & memories — Deleted when you delete your account or on request
  • Payment records — Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Analytics — Anonymized after 90 days

7. Data Security

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Regular security audits
  • No plain-text password storage (Supabase Auth handles hashing)

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

KelionAI is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified via email or in-app notification.
