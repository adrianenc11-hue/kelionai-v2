How to Exercise Your Rights

{{APP_NAME}} provides built-in tools to exercise your GDPR rights directly from the application:

You can manage your consent preferences for memory (AI learning), analytics, and marketing at any time. {{APP_NAME}} only uses essential cookies for authentication — no tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Data Controller
